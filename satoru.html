Why We Exist

The generation that built this nation now faces its hardest years alone. At Satoru Foundation, we believe aging isn't a burden it's a chapter that deserves dignity, belonging, and joy.

We work where elders actually live in neighbourhoods, homes, and community centres across Hyderabad. Our programs are shaped by research, delivered with heart, and built to last.

What do we do?

Here's a glimpse of the impactful work we do across various domains of care and community support.

Holistic Health & Well-being Initiatives

We enhance seniors' well-being through health camps, geriatric assessments, yoga, nutrition guidance, and awareness programs.

Social Engagement - Rebuilding Connections Initiatives

We combat loneliness by fostering intergenerational bonds through support groups, social events, and mental health services.

Digital & Financial Literacy Initiatives

We empower seniors with digital literacy and financial security through tech workshops, telemedicine, and financial planning education.

Rights awareness

We educate seniors about their rights and welfare schemes through legal awareness workshops and accessible, multilingual resources.
